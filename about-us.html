Rebuilding India's Scrap Economy

SetScrap is transforming the fragmented scrap industry into a transparent, technology-driven marketplace connecting verified buyers and suppliers across India.

Who We Are

SetScrap is a pan-India digital marketplace designed to streamline scrap procurement and selling. We connect scrap collectors, recyclers, traders, and manufacturers on one trusted platform with real-time pricing, verified supply, and reliable logistics.

India generates millions of tons of recyclable plastic and industrial scrap every year, yet the supply chain remains informal, fragmented, and inefficient. We exist to bring structure, price transparency, and scale to this massive opportunity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to formalize and digitize the scrap ecosystem using technology, trust, and data-driven pricing. We aim to create a marketplace where buyers get continuous quality supply at competitive prices and suppliers gain access to a larger, reliable customer base with faster payments.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional scrap procurement is plagued by price opacity, inconsistent quality, delayed payments, and unreliable logistics. Buyers struggle with supply continuity. Sellers struggle with limited market access and pricing power.

SetScrap eliminates these inefficiencies by creating a structured, transparent, and scalable marketplace infrastructure.
